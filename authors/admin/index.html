Hello! My name is Justine Macalindong and I am currently a senior majoring in Integrative Biology with a minor in Informatics at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. I am also an Undergraduate Researcher in the Harmon-Threatt Lab where I am working on my distinction project on changes in pollinator anti-predator behavior in areas of increased soil organic carbon.

Following graduation, I will be attending graduate school to obtain a master’s in bioinformatics. I hope to find a future career as a bioinformatician in either the biotechnology industry or government agencies.

Outside of academia, I like to play video games, play/write/listen to music, hang out with my friends, watch movies, and travel with my family.
